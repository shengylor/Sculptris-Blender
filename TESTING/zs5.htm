  • Can I Redo in Sculptris? Show/Hide
    Nope, so be sure to save your work often!

  • Why Does it Take Forever For Sculptris to go into Painting Mode? Show/Hide
    This is why I said to lower down your polycounts as much as you can!

  • Why does it have to take forever just to import OBJ files into Blender? Show/Hide
    Sculptris and Blender have different polycounts. If you model your character in Blender instead, you'll find less vertices to work and that makes life easier!

  • I want to do more in Blender, such as creating models, clothes, facial expression, background, lighting, etc. Show/Hide
    Yes you can, and they're all over the Internet, especially Youtube. So, check that out!
